FINN THE HUMAN

ABOUT FINN

Finn is the only human in the Land of Ooo. Finn swore that he would help anyone in need and often performs heroic feats to save the day.He would gladly fight or beat up anything evil, but would not harm anything good or "neutral".

CHARACTERISTICS

  1. - He is chaotic good!
  2. - He is super strong.
  3. - He owns a sword, a backpack and a hat.
  4. - His best friend is Jake the Dog.
  5. - He is friends to anyone good, leaves neutrals alone and he is enemy of evil!
